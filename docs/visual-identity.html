Animated loaders

Nine loaders, all riffing on the three-color triskelion. Pair indeterminate forms with short tasks; switch to the determinate ring when progress is knowable.
prefers-reduced-motion respected
01 · Mark spin1600ms · ease-in-out
Full-page splash. Use the actual logomark for brand presence on cold loads.
02 · Tri-arc spinner1400ms · linear
Default in-page indicator. 64px works inside cards; 24px works inside buttons.
03 · Bouncing dots1100ms · staggered
Inline messages, chat-style typing, low-priority background tasks.
04 · Linear sweep2100ms · ease
Top of cards/dialogs while content streams in. Hide once first paint completes.
05 · Triskelion ring2400ms · linear
Brand-forward spinner — picks up the three-blade motif of the mark.
06 · Concentric pulse2000ms · 3-stagger
Connection states, live indicators, ambient "thinking" surfaces.
07 · Tri-tint shimmer1800ms · ease-in-out
Skeleton placeholders for list rows and card content above the fold.
08 · Orbit trio2400ms · linear
Empty-state animations and long-running background syncs.
